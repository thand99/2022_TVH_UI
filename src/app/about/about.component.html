What do you need?

You will need your laptop/desktop and
internet connection to connect to the online event.

What Is TVH ?

The Tshwane Varsity Hackthon is a hacking competition that is
organised by the Tshwane University of Technology's faculty of
Information and Communication Technology and the Technology and Research & Innovation office.

On the day of the hackathon, participants will be requested to come up
with solutions to certain real-life problems by using technology.
Each teams will be allocated a mentor/coach to work with during the duration of the Hackthon.

Who Can Participate ?

The Tshwane Varsity Hackthon is open to students from TUT,UP,SMU and UNISA.

Application Fee?

Joining the Tshwane Varsity Hackthon is Completely Free!

How will teams be formed ?

The teams will be formed during the workshop day
